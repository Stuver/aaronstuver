Mission

Purpose Beliefs Principles

Purpose

I will love and be in awe of God's creation, the relationship/wisdom of the Holy Spirit, and salvation through Jesus' death and resurrection.
And I will bring glory to God today and for all eternity.

Beliefs

I believe God created the Heavens and the Earth. I believe He designed Creation to be perfect, but humanity denied the perfection of God’s design—sinned—and was separated from Him. God sent Jesus as a living sacrifice. He was betrayed, killed on a cross, and resurrected. His sacrifice served as a final sacrifice.

Whoever believes in Him will not perish but is forgiven and will be reunited with God for eternity. Because I have accepted this gift, I believe I will be taken with Christ when He returns again, and if I should die before that time, I believe I will be accepted into God’s kingdom and dwell with Him, bringing Him glory for eternity.

This is my mission: to love and be in awe of God’s creation, the relationship/wisdom of the Holy Spirit, and my salvation through Jesus’ death and resurrection and to bring glory to God today and for all eternity.

Principles

I will bring glory to God’s creation by…
…maintaining physical fitness as to be capable of serving others and enjoying athletic activity.
…trusting God with my physique and honoring God’s design for sex—no vanity.
…enjoying art in an attitude of worship and celebration of Creation. …being intentional in what I consume, striving to support those who care for Creation.

I will grow in God’s wisdom through… …fellowship with believers who will keep me accountable and I can share life with.
…making time daily to learn more about God and His Word.
…being intentional in my thoughts, speech, and actions.

I will show my love for Jesus and gratitude for His sacrifice by…
…serving others with enthusiasm and quality, investing in their eternal lives and putting them first in all I do.
…reflecting the transformative power of the Gospel in my life, giving all glory to God.
…not being ashamed of my mission (the Gospel).

I will be organized and proactive in order to perform my best and give my full concentration to the activity at hand for the “other” (group or person) at hand.
